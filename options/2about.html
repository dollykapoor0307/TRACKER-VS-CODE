About Fitness Tracking

Tracking fitness helps you stay motivated, maintain a healthy lifestyle, and achieve your goals. Here’s why it’s important and how you can do it:

Water Intake

Drinking adequate water keeps you hydrated, boosts metabolism, and aids in digestion. Click the image to watch a video on the importance of hydration.

Body Mass Index (BMI)

BMI is a simple calculation of body weight and height to determine if you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. Monitoring BMI helps you maintain a healthy body weight.

Step Count

Tracking daily steps motivates you to stay active. Aim for 10,000 steps a day for a healthier life. Click the image to learn more about staying active!
